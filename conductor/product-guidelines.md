# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style

User-facing documentation and in-app text should adopt a **Clear and Concise** prose style. This means the language used should be easy to understand, direct, and free of unnecessary jargon. The goal is to convey information efficiently and effectively to the user.

## Brand Messaging

The brand messaging should be **Empowering and Innovative**. This involves focusing on user agency and highlighting the cutting-edge solutions the product provides. The message should inspire users and demonstrate the product's forward-thinking approach.

## Visual Identity and User Experience (UX)

The visual identity and user experience of the product should be guided by the principles of **Simplicity and Intuition** and **Consistency and Predictability**.

-   **Simplicity and Intuition:** The design should prioritize ease of navigation and understanding, minimizing cognitive load for the user. Interfaces should be straightforward and self-explanatory.
-   **Consistency and Predictability:** Uniformity in design elements, interactions, and behaviors across the entire application is crucial. This ensures a predictable user experience, making the product easier to learn and use over time.
